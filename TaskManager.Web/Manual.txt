Task Manager Manual
===================

Welcome to Task Manager — a simple and efficient way to keep all your to-dos organized in one place.

Add a Task
----------
To add a new task:
- Enter the Title of the task.
- Select a Due Date — make sure it’s a future date.
- Provide a brief Description of what needs to be done.
- Click the 'Add Task' button to save it.
Your new task will appear in the task list once added.

Mark as Complete
----------------
When you finish a task:
- Locate the task in your list.
- Click the 'Complete' button next to it.
The task status will then change to Completed.
